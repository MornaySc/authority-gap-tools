Executive Authority Gap Baseline
The Gap Between What You Deliver and What Moves Your Career.
You have invested in coaching. Personal development. Skills and leadership training. All of it. And you are still stuck in the same role, same place.
It is not because you are not good enough. It is because nobody ever showed you how the room works.
Here we map where the break is. In one session we build your next move.

This baseline maps your position across five dimensions. At the end you will see exactly where the structural break is and what needs to change. Answer from where you are, not where you want to be.

The Five Advancement Signals
Dimension 1
Contribution Visibility
Whether the people who control what happens next can see your work as yours
Dimension 2
Attribution Stability
Whether credit is building an arc toward the next level, or a floor that holds you in place
Dimension 3
Solution Posture
Whether you arrive with answers before being asked, and own problems before being directed
Dimension 4
Contribution Articulation
Whether you can name what you specifically did with enough precision that no one else could claim it
Dimension 5
Trajectory Clarity
Whether you can read your own position clearly enough to move deliberately

Final step
Part Three - Your account

In your own words

Something is not working. You know it. You have probably known it for a while.

This is not the place for the polished version - not how you would explain it to HR, not how you would frame it in a performance review. This is the place for what is happening, as clearly as you can say it.

Write about the specific moments that have stayed with you. The conversation that did not go the way it should have. The result that landed without your name on it. The room you were not in. Write about what you have tried, and what happened. What you think is driving it. What you want - not in abstract terms, but specifically.

Minimum 400 words. No maximum. The gaps, the frustrations, the things that do not make sense - those are exactly what is useful here.
